About Our Kannada Rajyotsava Celebration

Tismo's Second Floor team proudly presents "Festivals of Karnataka" as part of Ethnic Day @ Tismo 2025. Kannada Rajyotsava, celebrated on November 1st, commemorates the formation of the state of Karnataka in 1956, marking the unification of all Kannada-speaking regions under one state.

Karnataka is blessed with incredible diversity - from the coastal beauty of Mangalore to the technological prowess of Bengaluru, from the royal heritage of Mysore to the ancient temples of Hampi. Each region contributes its unique festivals, traditions, and cultural flavor to the rich tapestry of Karnataka.
