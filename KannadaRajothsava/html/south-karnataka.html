About South Karnataka

South Karnataka, encompassing the Mysuru Region, covers the districts of Mysuru, Mandya, Hassan, and Chamarajanagara. This region represents the cultural and historical heart of Karnataka and was the seat of the powerful Mysore Kingdom and the legendary Tipu Sultan.

Known for its magnificent palaces, beautiful gardens, and rich artistic traditions, this region is famous for Mysore silk sarees, sandalwood products, and traditional sweets like Mysore Pak. The region's cuisine includes specialties like Ragi Mudde, Bisi Bele Bath, and various coconut-based preparations.

From the grandeur of Mysuru Palace to the serenity of Brindavan Gardens, from the historical town of Srirangapatna to the wildlife reserves of Bandipur and Nagarhole, South Karnataka offers a perfect blend of royal heritage, natural beauty, and spiritual significance spanning across all four districts.
