About South-East Karnataka

South-East Karnataka, representing the Bengaluru/Old Mysore Expansion Region, encompasses the districts of Bengaluru, Ramanagara, Tumakuru, and Kolar. This region represents the contemporary face of Karnataka, blending traditional culture with modern technology and urban development.

Known as the Silicon Valley of India, Bengaluru is the IT capital that has put Karnataka on the global map. The region's cuisine features traditional favorites like Bisi Bele Bath, Rava Idli, and Mysore Masala Dosa, alongside international flavors reflecting its cosmopolitan nature. The area is famous for its gardens, pleasant climate, and vibrant nightlife.

From the bustling tech parks of Bengaluru to the silk-producing centers of Kolar, from the coconut groves of Tumakuru to the film city of Ramanagaram, South-East Karnataka showcases how tradition and modernity can coexist harmoniously across all four districts in the new millennium.
