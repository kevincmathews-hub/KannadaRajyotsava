About Central Karnataka

Central Karnataka, encompassing the Malnad Region, covers the districts of Shivamogga, Chikkamagaluru, and Davanagere. This region, literally meaning "rain country," is nestled in the Western Ghats and known for its lush green landscapes, coffee plantations, and misty mountains.

Famous for producing some of India's finest coffee, this region is dotted with sprawling plantations of coffee and spices in Chikkamagaluru. The region's cuisine features unique dishes like Akki Rotti, Bamboo Shoot curry, and various preparations using jackfruit, colocasia, and other local produce.

From the majestic Jog Falls in Shivamogga to the coffee estates of Chikkamagaluru, from the industrial town of Davanagere known for its cotton and benne dosa to the wildlife sanctuaries, Central Karnataka offers breathtaking natural beauty combined with rich cultural heritage and diverse economic activities.
