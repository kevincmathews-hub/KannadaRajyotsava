About Coastal Karnataka

Coastal Karnataka, representing the Karavali/Coastal Belt, is a stunning region that stretches along the Arabian Sea coast. This region encompasses the districts of Udupi, Dakshina Kannada, and Uttara Kannada, each offering unique cultural traditions, pristine beaches, and rich heritage.

Known for its distinctive cuisine featuring coconut, seafood, and aromatic spices, Coastal Karnataka is home to famous dishes like Neer Dosa, Kori Rotti, and Ghee Roast. The region celebrates vibrant festivals like Yakshagana performances, Kambala buffalo races, and traditional Bhuta Kola rituals.

From the temple town of Udupi to the beaches of Gokarna, from the historical forts of Bekal to the Western Ghats' lush forests, Coastal Karnataka offers an incredible blend of spirituality, nature, and cultural richness that makes it truly special.
